About Us

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department was established in 2014 as the first and only engineering department at the University of Barisal. Starting from the university's third batch, CSE was one of its six founding departments. To date, approximately 600 students have studied or graduated from this department."

Our mission is to prepare students for successful careers in computing through a rigorous curriculum, hands-on learning experiences.
